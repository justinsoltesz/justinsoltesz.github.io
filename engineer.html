Academic projects

Grove City Shared-use Path: Our project team studied possible means of connecting Fryer Park to Mount Carmel facility in Grove City, Ohio, which included providing a crossing of I-71. Investigated right-of-way, quality of connection, cost, permitting, environmental and floodplain impacts, and maintenance of traffic and traffic impacts. Included oral presentation and final report of findings.

4th Street Exit from I-670: Our project team is currently studying possible realignments of the 4th Street exit from I-670 west just north of Downtown Columbus. The project's goal is to slow traffic on 4th Street, especially the traffic coming off of I-670, as significant residential development is occurring in the area.
